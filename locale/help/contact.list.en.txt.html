personal list of contacts

In order to find other members more quickly, you can add them to your personal list of contacts. On the profile page of the respective member you will find a link for doing so. If you publish a contact, others can see this member as listed on your list of contacts.

Important: For improved ease-of-use your list of contacts will be shown to you when you delegate. In order to delegate to a member, you must first add him or her to your contacts. Removing a contact does not revoke the delegation, though.
